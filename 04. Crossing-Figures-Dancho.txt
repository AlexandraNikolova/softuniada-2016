Crossing Figures (Simple Geometry)
----------------------------------

We are given a rectangle and circle. Find their relative position:
 - non-crossing
 - crossing
 - circle inside rectangle
 - rectangle inside circle

Example:
circle(3, 3, 5)
rectangle(10, 5, -3, 12)

Example:
rectangle(5, -5, 0, 20)
circle(-5, 12, 15)


Simple parsing + simple logic (basic geometry)

Level: 4/10
