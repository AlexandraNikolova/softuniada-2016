3-Partition Problem
-------------------

3 brothers: Alan, Bob, Charley need to split presents. Split N numbers into 3 equal subsets.

{5 1 8 3 2 3 2} -> {5 3} {8} {1 2 2 3}


Input:
5 1 8 3 2 3 2
Output:
5+3
8
1+2+2+3

Dynamic programming with 2-parameter function.
Level: 5/10
